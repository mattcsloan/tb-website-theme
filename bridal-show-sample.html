Show Features

Fashion Shows

See the hottest wedding fashions for you and your entire bridal party on stage! All fashion shows include several bridal shops, tuxedo companies and florists to give you the widest array of options to help find your perfect look.

Shows last approximately 40 minutes. General admission seating only.

Schedule: 12:00PM & 3:00PM located in the Upstairs Ballroom

Featuring: David’s Bridal, Lavender Bridal Salon, American Commodore Dress, Men’s Wearhouse, American Commodore Tuxedo, Tuxedo Junction, Perfect Petals Design ... and more!

Just Added: A Moms and ‘Maids fashion show

Who says you have to be a bride to get jacked for a wedding? Our midday fashion show will showcase the unspoken heroes of the wedding – moms and ‘maids! Watch as real moms waltz down the runway wearing designer gowns alongside diverse and trendy bridesmaid gowns. The bride may be the star of the wedding day, but this fashion show will give moms and ‘maids their chance to say Yes to the Dress!

Time: 1:30pm
